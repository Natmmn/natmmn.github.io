    Net Art

    Artist Statement: My concept of this project was to create a choose your own adventure story about a fearless Knight having to go save The Princess from The Evil Wizard. As you click through your options to get closer to finding her, there are times where you can turn back and go home or keep going. I wanted this project to be a bit comedic to make it more interesting. Enjoy! :D
