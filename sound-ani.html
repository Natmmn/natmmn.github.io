Project 2 | Animation with Sound

Artist Statement: An animated version of my "Remix Monatage", showing how social media now a days is lacking in privacy. Everyone can figure out where you are on a map, even people who are on "Ghost Mode". This is a scary topic and I created it in a fun-colorful way because that is exactly how Snapchat portrays it as. The sound I added in helps convey my concept by letting people know where to look first.
