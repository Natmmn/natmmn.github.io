Project 1 | Remix Montage

Artist Statement: My montage's concept is about the lack of privacy in social media today. Everyone, friends and strangers alike, know your location, even when a persons location is private, they still know where you are at. This is a scary concept, but I wanted to portray it in a fun and colorful way because that is how snapchat, and any other social networking sites, convey it as.
