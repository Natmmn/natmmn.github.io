    3D Landscape

    Artist Statement: I wanted my 3D Landscape to include structures people can easily recognize, so I included Big Bed, The London Eye, and The London Bridge. To manipulate the London setting I sampled a mountain range from California from the internet and placed the London structures on it. I realized that the mountain range looked very similar to water, so I changed my concept from London in a Mountain to London Underwater. When I printed it out I painted the water in various shades of blue to show depth and the buildings in a taupe color.
