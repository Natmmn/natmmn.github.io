You open the door revealing The Princess dressed in all white. You tell her that you are here to save her from the wretched Evil Wizard, but she frowns and tells you that she was never kidnapped. She ran away because her and the Wizard fell in love and wanted to get married. Today is their wedding day, too bad it was not yours. You go back to the Kingdom and tell The King that you never found his daughter. Out of anger he banishes you. So really, The Wizard and The Princess lived happily ever after. The End.
